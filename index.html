Dairy Milk is a British brand of milk chocolate manufactured by Cadbury.
It was introduced in the United Kingdom in June 1905 and now consists of a number of products.
In 1928, Cadbury's introduced the "glass and a half" slogan to accompany the Dairy Milk chocolate bar, to advertise the bar's higher milk content
